# Business Info

## The Business

- **Name:** SOSA — Save Our Species Alliance
- **What it does:** A for-profit media agency and conservation trip operator that helps NGOs worldwide tell their story and find funding, while running hands-on conservation experiences that let everyday people become active conservationists.
- **Industry:** Conservation media, wildlife storytelling, experiential travel
- **Stage:** Early-stage startup. Not yet profitable. Funded personally by founder Blake Moynes.
- **Non-profit side:** In progress — working towards launching alongside the for-profit arm.

## Products / Services

- **Conservation media production:** Filming, editing, and content creation for NGO partners worldwide
- **SOSA experiences / trips:** Curated conservation adventures where guests join NGO research, get hands-on with wildlife, and learn about conservation work firsthand
- **Content strategy & distribution:** Instagram, YouTube, TikTok channels telling conservation stories to grow awareness and funding for NGO partners

## Customers

- **NGOs and non-profits:** Wildlife conservation organisations globally — sharks (Maldives, Florida), jaguars and sloths (South America), lions and elephants (Africa), and more
- **Everyday people:** Anyone who wants a meaningful adventure — guests on SOSA trips who want to feel like genuine conservationists, not just observers
- **How they find SOSA:** Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, word of mouth from past trip guests, and Blake Moynes' public profile (gained significant following from appearing on The Bachelor)

## Founder

- **Blake Moynes** — founder and face of SOSA. Gained public profile from The Bachelor. Funded SOSA by selling his house and using TV earnings. Actively involved as the brand figurehead.
